Tenner: A Freelance Service-for-Hire Website

A simple E-commerce website inspired by the freelance for hire site - Fiverr. The goal of the project was to provide a platform for freelance workers to market their skills to a wider audience. Services provided were digital-based and available upon completion. We also integrated Stripe into our payment options, as it is secure and easy to use.

Technologies: Python, Django, HTML, CSS

Link to Repo

JamPacked: The Event Planning Platform

JamPacked is an event planning web application designed for planning social events like parties, trips, weddings and other social gatherings. This application is designed to make planning more manageable and accessible and make planning events a fun activity that can be done with others. It aims to take the stress out of event planning, allowing users to easily access event details, plans, tasks and RSVP for events on a dedicated platform. The core of this application is easy and fun collaboration, keeping the user experience at the highest priority.

Technologies: MERN Stack

2024 Tallaght Hospital Transforming Healthcare Hackathon Winner

I had the great opportunity to come back for my third and final Hackathon with a team of my wonderful and determined friends; Noor Isha Nadeem, Moyin Olusona and Sayali Mote. We were presented with a challenging, real-world problem, preventing the formation of pressure ulcers in patients. Our solution involved creating a new fabric with micro-bit technology, allowing for monitoring of pressure build up in different parts of the body. Due to our collective efforts, we ended up winning the overall prize, which was such an amazing achievement for our last year in TU Dublin!
